About Our Games

Welcome to Gamers Den: The Loot Cave, where classic games meet modern technology. We've reimagined your favorite childhood games with beautiful graphics, smooth animations, and intelligent AI opponents.

Tic Tac Toe

A classic strategy game where you play against an intelligent AI. The AI uses the minimax algorithm to make optimal moves, making it a challenging opponent. Try to get three of your marks in a row, column, or diagonal to win!

  • Play against AI with unbeatable strategy
  • Track your wins and losses
  • Simple yet engaging gameplay
  • Instant game switching

Pac-Man

Navigate through the maze, collect dots, and avoid the ghosts! This classic arcade game features smooth controls, multiple ghosts with different behaviors, and a scoring system. Can you clear the board without getting caught?

  • Classic maze navigation gameplay
  • Multiple ghosts with AI behavior
  • Power pellets and scoring system
  • Keyboard and touch controls

Chess

The ultimate strategy game! Play chess with full piece movement rules, including special moves like pawn promotion. Each piece has unique movement patterns, and the goal is to checkmate your opponent's king.

  • Complete chess rules implementation
  • All piece movements and captures
  • Visual move indicators
  • Checkmate and stalemate detection
